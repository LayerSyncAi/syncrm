# SynCRM — Product Benefits & Guided Walkthrough

### A purpose-built CRM for real estate teams that close deals

*Prepared for prospective partners evaluating SynCRM*

---

## 1. The 60-second pitch

Real estate teams lose deals in the cracks — a hot buyer who never got a callback, a duplicate lead worked by two agents, a property match nobody made, a commission split argued over after the fact. Generic CRMs (or spreadsheets and WhatsApp groups) weren't built for how property agencies actually work.

**SynCRM is.** It's a real-time CRM built around the property sales workflow: capture a lead, score it, match it to inventory, work it through a visual pipeline, share deals between agents, and pay out commissions automatically when it closes — all live across the whole team, with no page refreshes and no data falling through the gaps.

> **One line:** *Sync your team. Close more deals.*

## 3. Why SynCRM is different

- **Real-time, not "refresh to see changes."** Built on Convex, every action — a stage change, a new note, a closed deal — appears instantly for every team member. A live "● Live" indicator shows the data is always current.
- **Built for property, not retrofitted.** Pipeline stages, buy/rent interest, preferred areas, viewings, mandates, proof-of-funds folders, and commission scenarios are first-class concepts — not custom fields you have to bolt on.
- **An AI Copilot inside the app.** Agents can ask questions in plain language ("Which of my leads haven't been contacted this week?") and get answers grounded in their actual data.
- **Collaboration over competition.** Property sharing and automated commission splits make agent-to-agent deals seamless and fair.
- **Local market ready.** Direct import from **PropertyBook** (propertybook.co.zw) pulls live listings into your inventory, with a nightly refresh of price and status.
- **Fully white-label.** Your name, logo, colours, and domain — SynCRM becomes *your* product. (See §11.)
- **Secure by design.** Multi-tenant isolation, role-based access, and an audit trail on sensitive actions.

## 4. Guided walkthrough

This is the tour we'd give your team on day one. Follow it as a demo script.

### Stop 1 — The Dashboard (`/app/dashboard`)
*"Here's your whole business at a glance, updating live."*

- **Win Rate** front and centre, plus animated KPI cards: Total / Open / Won / Lost leads.
- **Lead Score Distribution** — every lead bucketed Cold → Cool → Warm → Hot → On Fire, so you instantly see how much real opportunity is in the pipe.
- **Average Score by Stage** — reveals where good leads stall.
- **Top Unworked Leads** — high-scoring leads with no activity in 7+ days. *This single panel stops hot leads from going cold.*
- **Leads by Stage** — pipeline health and bottlenecks in one bar chart.

> **Benefit:** Managers stop asking "where are we?" — the answer is always on screen.

### Stop 2 — Capturing & working a lead (`/app/leads`)
*"Every opportunity in one place, prioritized for you."*

- A filterable, sortable leads table — by stage, interest (buy/rent), score band, location, owner, or free-text search.
- **Change a lead's stage inline** from a dropdown — no opening screens.
- Create a lead with full context: contact, interest, source, budget range (multi-currency), preferred areas, notes — and **attach matching properties right from the form**. Select several listings and SynCRM creates one linked lead per property for a buyer interested in multiple homes.
- **Duplicate detection runs automatically** as you pick a contact, so you never start a second file on the same person.

> **Benefit:** Agents spend time selling, not hunting through tabs.

### Stop 3 — The lead detail page (`/app/leads/[id]`)
*"Everything about this deal, on one page."*

- A hero card with the essentials; a **Timeline** of every call, WhatsApp, email, meeting, viewing, and note.
- **Matched Properties** and AI-powered **Suggested** properties with match scores and side-by-side comparison.
- **Documents** tab with real estate-specific folders: Mandates to Sell, Contracts, ID Copies, Proof of Funds.
- When a deal is **won**, capture deal value and reason — and SynCRM offers to close sibling leads on the same property as lost, keeping the pipeline honest.

> **Benefit:** Full deal history and paperwork in one click — nothing lives in someone's inbox.

### Stop 4 — Smart matching & inventory (`/app/properties`)
*"The right property in front of the right buyer, automatically."*

- Manage your full inventory in table or gallery view, with photos, status flow (Available → Under Offer → Sold/Let), and document folders.
- **Auto-suggestions** score each property against a lead on interest type, budget, location, and availability.
- **Bulk Match** several leads to inventory at once.
- **Import from PropertyBook** to populate inventory from live market listings in minutes, refreshed nightly.

> **Benefit:** Faster, more relevant responses to buyers — the difference between winning and losing a deal.

### Stop 5 — Scoring & prioritization (`/app/admin/lead-scoring`)
*"Tell SynCRM what a good lead looks like, and it ranks them all."*

- Admins configure **weighted criteria** (has budget, recent activity, preferred areas, activity count, deal-size threshold, and more), each toggleable with its own weight.
- A **live preview** scores any lead as you tune the dials — no save-and-check loop.
- **Recompute all scores** in one click; scores then drive the dashboard, list badges, and filters.

> **Benefit:** Every agent works the highest-value leads first — measurable lift in conversion.

### Stop 6 — Collaboration & commissions (`/app/admin/commissions`)
*"Deals between agents, done right and paid fairly."*

- **Property sharing** lets a listing agent hand a property to a colleague's specific lead and work the deal together, with clear statuses.
- On close, SynCRM **auto-calculates the commission split** from admin-defined scenarios — shared deal, own-property/own-lead, or company property — with a Pending → Approved → Paid workflow.

> **Benefit:** No more spreadsheet commission math or end-of-month disputes.

### Stop 7 — Tasks, reminders & the AI Copilot
*"Nothing slips, and answers are a sentence away."*

- A daily **Tasks** action centre with Due Today / Overdue / Upcoming / Completed, and a visual urgency ring on every task.
- Automated **email reminders**: pre-reminders, a daily digest, and overdue alerts — deduped so no spam.
- The **SynCRM Copilot** answers natural-language questions about your own pipeline and helps agents act faster.

> **Benefit:** Follow-ups happen on time, every time — the #1 driver of close rates.

## 5. A day in the life

**Maria (Agent), 9:05 AM.** Opens the dashboard. "Top Unworked Leads" flags a hot buyer she hasn't called in eight days. One click to the lead, full history on screen, she calls and logs it — score ticks up.

**Maria, 11:30 AM.** New website enquiry. She creates the lead; duplicate detection shows the buyer already exists, so she works the existing file instead of starting a second. The "Recommended" filter surfaces three matching listings; she attaches them and books a viewing.

**Tom (Listing Agent), 2:00 PM.** Has the apartment Maria's buyer wants. He shares it to her lead. They co-work the deal.

**They close, Friday.** Maria marks the lead Won with the deal value. SynCRM splits the commission automatically — property agent, lead agent, company — and the record enters the approval flow.

**Priya (Admin), Monday.** The dashboard already shows the win, updated conversion rate, and commission pending approval. No status meeting required.

## 7. Onboarding — fast time to value

1. **Sign up** — the first user becomes the org admin; the organization is created automatically.
2. **Import leads** via CSV with column auto-mapping, duplicate handling, and a preview before commit.
3. **Import inventory** from PropertyBook (or CSV/manual).
4. **Configure** your pipeline stages, scoring weights, and commission scenarios.
5. **Invite agents** — they get a guided interactive tour on first login.

Most teams are live on real data the same day.

---

## 8. Security, access & trust

- **Role-based access** — agents see only their own leads and contacts; admins see the whole org. Sensitive actions (user management, commissions, lead merge) are admin-only.
- **Multi-tenant isolation** — each organization's data is fully separated.
- **Audit trail** on merges and other sensitive operations.
- **Authentication** with email/password, password reset, and admin-forced password changes.
- **Secure document storage** scoped to your organization.
